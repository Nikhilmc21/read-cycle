Post an Item

Our website serves as a centralized platform where users can input details of their lost items, ranging from personal belongings to electronic devices, and valuable documents. To initiate the process, you can create an account, to ensure security and facilitate easy tracking of lost item reports. Once you are logged in, you can fill out a comprehensive form detailing the lost item's description, location, time of loss, and any other relevant information that might aid in the search.

Responsible Posting

At Finders Keepers, we value responsible posting by our users. We kindly ask you to be considerate and provide accurate information when sharing lost items. This responsible approach helps maintain the credibility and reliability of our platform, ensuring that others can trust the information provided.

Clear Descriptions

When posting items, please take the time to craft clear and detailed descriptions. Providing truthful and comprehensive information not only helps us build a more robust database but also greatly assists others in identifying and recovering their lost belongings. Your efforts in this regard are highly appreciated.

Respectful Behavior

We strive to create a respectful and supportive community. In your interactions with fellow users, we encourage you to uphold respectful behavior. Positive and considerate communication not only makes our platform a more pleasant place but also fosters a sense of unity among our users.

Helpful Reporting

If you ever encounter content that you believe is inappropriate or goes against our guidelines, we urge you to contact us. Your active involvement in identifying and reporting such content plays a vital role in maintaining a safe and respectful online environment. We greatly value your contributions to the overall integrity of our platform.

FAQs

How can I post about a lost item on the website?

If you have lost an item, login with your account and scroll to the bottom of the page. Fill in the form to find lost items which match the description of your post.

What happens if someone loses an expensive item?

If you lose an item of high value such as a phone or a laptop, contact us using the form at the bottom of this page and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

What happens if an item is not claimed?

Posts remain active for 6 months. If an item is not claimed within this time frame, it will be donated to a local charity or the support staff in school.

How to report a lost item?

To report a lost item, please hand it over to our team at the Lost and Found, located in the lunch hall.
